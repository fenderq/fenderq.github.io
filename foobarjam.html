FooBarJam 2020

What is this?

2020-09-01 willc asked ...

Can we work on a small project, ie channel website, as a team and help me better understand how multiple people work together on a project?

This is a creative jam to produce something we can all participate in. It is meant to be easy, simple and not stressful.

We will vote on one of the themes:

  • Cyberpunk city under the sea
  • Lost treasures in the jungle
  • The secret moon base +2 votes

The jam will be divided into the following tasks:

  • Image to represent the theme
  • Audio file which will loop
  • Super short story
  • Web page to put it all together

Why are we doing this?

To see how well we can work as a team and to practice being creative.

When are we doing this?

You must have signed up and voted by September 13.

We must finish by September 20.
